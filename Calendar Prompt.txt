Colors of the day for Doomsdays: (Thai tradition)

- Monday - #FFFF00 (Yellow - used for Easter Vigil and Easter Octave)

Black is defined: #000000

Liturgical color uses:
Green (#008000) - Ordinary Time
Violet (#800080) - Advent and Lent, All Souls and Funerals
Rose (#ff0080) - 3rd Sunday of Advent and 4th Sunday of Lent
White (#ffffff) - Christmastide and Eastertide, Maundy Thursday, Feasts of the Lord, all confessors, Saint John the Evangelist, Nativity of John the Evangelist
Red (#da251d) - Feasts of the Passion of the Lord (e.g. Palm Sunday, Good Friday, Exaltation of the Cross) Pentecost, Martys, Apostles, Evangelists, Papal Funerals
Black (#000000) - Holy Saturday proper
Yellow (#ffff00) - Easter Vigil and Easter Octave
Blue (#3399ff) - Feasts of Virgin Mary, New Year's Day
Red+Green: Christmas Octave (except 26-28 December falling on weekdays)
